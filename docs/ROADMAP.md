## Dependencies & Risks

### Technical Risks
- AI model performance degradation
- Scalability challenges at high volume
- Data privacy compliance complexity

**Mitigation**: Regular model retraining, load testing, compliance audits

### Market Risks
- Increased competition
- Market adoption slower than expected
- Economic downturn affecting hiring

**Mitigation**: Early market entry, strong product-market fit, flexible pricing

### Execution Risks
- Team attrition
- Resource constraints
- Scope creep

**Mitigation**: Strong company culture, clear prioritization, agile methodology
